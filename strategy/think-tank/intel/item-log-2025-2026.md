## How to Read This Log

Each item has been reviewed and given a recommendation. The **Disposition** section is intentionally blank — that's for the named approver to fill after reviewing the recommendation. Items are grouped by the quarter they were identified, matching the chronological order a real CU would process them.

**Recommendation key**:
- **incorporate** — act on it now: update a specific CANVAS file
- **defer-to-cycle** — real finding, needs stress testing first
- **monitoring** — signal is real but not yet actionable; set a review trigger
- **dismiss** — not relevant or already covered
