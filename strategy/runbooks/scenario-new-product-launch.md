# Runbook: New Product Launch

> **Mode**: CANVAS-Full | **Duration**: 8-16 weeks | **Agents**: All | **Lead**: CEO + Compliance Officer

---

## Scenario

The credit union is launching a new product — a new loan product, deposit account, card program, digital service, or financial wellness offering. Every new product must pass regulatory review, receive board approval, and be supported by trained staff and compliant marketing before going live.

## When to Activate

- Launching a new loan product (personal, auto, HELOC, business line of credit)
- Launching a new deposit product (certificate special, money market tier, HSA)
- Launching a new card program (credit card, debit reward, secured card)
- Launching a digital banking feature (P2P payments, real-time alerts, new mobile feature)
- Expanding membership eligibility to a new field of membership
- Entering a new market or opening a new branch

## Product Launch Timeline

### Phase 1: Product Definition and Regulatory Review (Weeks 1-4)

```
WEEK 1: Product Concept and Business Case
├── CEO: Define strategic rationale — why this product, why now, which members it serves
├── CFO: Initial financial modeling — cost of funds, pricing, margin, volume projections
├── Marketing Manager: Member needs assessment — demand signals, competitive analysis
├── Risk Manager: Initial risk assessment — credit risk, operational risk, concentration risk
└── CEO: Business case presented to senior leadership for concept approval

WEEK 2: Regulatory and Compliance Assessment
├── Compliance Officer: Full regulatory mapping for new product
│   ├── TILA/Reg Z: If a loan product — all disclosure requirements
│   ├── NCUA Part 707 / Reg DD: If a deposit product — Truth in Savings
│   ├── FCBA / Reg Z Part 1026 Subpart B: If a credit card product
│   ├── UDAP/UDAAP: All marketing and member communications
│   ├── ECOA/Reg B: If lending — fair lending implications
│   └── BSA/AML: Member type and product-specific AML risk
├── BSA Officer: AML risk assessment for new product
├── Compliance Officer: Identify required disclosures and operational controls
└── Compliance Officer: Risk assessment memo to CEO — proceed / modify / do not proceed

WEEK 3: Product Design Finalization
├── CFO: Final pricing approved by ALCO — rate, fee structure, margins
├── Loan Officer (if loan): Underwriting criteria — eligibility, DTI, LTV, exceptions
├── Deposit Operations Manager (if deposit): Account structure, rate tiers, penalties
├── Card Services Specialist (if card): Card network selection, program terms, fraud controls
├── Core Systems Administrator: Core system configuration feasibility assessment
└── Risk Manager: Stress test on financial projections — base, adverse, severely adverse scenarios

WEEK 4: Board Approval Package
├── CEO: Prepare board product approval memo
│   ├── Product description and member benefit
│   ├── Financial projections and margin analysis
│   ├── Compliance Officer regulatory assessment
│   ├── Risk Manager risk assessment
│   └── Implementation timeline
├── CFO: Board-ready financial model
├── Compliance Officer: Regulatory clearance memo
└── CEO: Present product for board approval — motion and vote required
```

### Phase 2: Build and Configure (Weeks 5-10)

```
WEEK 5-6: Systems and Procedures
├── Core Systems Administrator: Core system product configuration
│   ├── Set up product codes, rate tables, fee schedules
│   ├── Configure required disclosure document generation
│   ├── Configure reporting and monitoring fields
│   └── Test end-to-end transaction workflow in test environment
├── Compliance Officer: Draft all required member-facing disclosures
│   ├── TILA disclosure (loan products)
│   ├── Truth in Savings disclosure (deposit products)
│   ├── Card member agreement (card products)
│   └── Privacy notice addendum (if new data uses)
└── Deposit Operations Manager / Loan Officer: Draft operational procedures

WEEK 7-8: Marketing and Communications
├── Marketing Manager: Develop member-facing marketing materials
│   ├── Rate/feature promotion collateral
│   ├── Direct mail or email campaign
│   ├── Website product page
│   └── Branch point-of-sale materials
├── Compliance Officer: UDAP/UDAAP review of all marketing materials
│   ├── No deceptive terms, rates, or conditions
│   ├── APR/APYE disclosed per regulatory requirements
│   ├── Required disclosure language included
│   └── Fair lending: No materials that exclude or discourage protected classes
└── Marketing Manager: Revise based on compliance review; re-submit for clearance

WEEK 9-10: Staff Training
├── CHRO: Develop training program for all member-facing staff
├── Loan Officer / Deposit Operations Manager: Create product knowledge training content
├── Compliance Officer: Compliance training module — disclosures, UDAP requirements, new procedures
├── Branch Manager: Coordinate training delivery to all branch staff
├── Member Services Representative: Front-line scripts and FAQs prepared
└── CHRO: Track training completion — 100% required before launch
```

### Phase 3: Pre-Launch Review (Weeks 11-12)

```
WEEK 11: Internal Audit Pre-Launch Review
├── Internal Auditor: Independent pre-launch review
│   ├── Systems configuration tested against product spec
│   ├── Disclosure accuracy and completeness verified
│   ├── Operational procedures adequate for compliance
│   └── Staff training completion verified
└── Internal Auditor: Pre-launch review report issued to CEO and Compliance Officer

WEEK 12: Final Readiness Gate
├── Core Systems Administrator: Production environment tested — all product workflows
├── Compliance Officer: Final disclosure review — final version approved
├── Branch Manager: Branch readiness confirmed — staff trained and prepared
├── Marketing Manager: All launch materials printed, approved, and ready
├── CFO: Pricing confirmed current and approved by ALCO
└── CEO: GO / NO-GO decision for launch date
```

### Phase 4: Launch and Monitoring (Weeks 13-16)

```
LAUNCH DAY
├── Core Systems Administrator: Activate product in core system
├── Marketing Manager: Launch campaign activated (email, branch materials, website)
├── Branch Manager: Staff briefing — product is live; member questions expected
├── Member Services Representative: Begin accepting applications / opening accounts
└── Compliance Officer: Launch day monitoring — any disclosure or procedure issues?

WEEKS 13-16: Post-Launch Monitoring
├── CFO: Weekly volume and margin tracking vs. projection
├── Loan Officer / Deposit Operations Manager: Application/opening volume tracking
├── Compliance Officer: Monitor member complaints — any UDAP concerns?
├── BSA Officer: Monitor transaction patterns — any AML red flags in new product?
├── Core Systems Administrator: System performance monitoring — errors, exceptions
└── Marketing Manager: Campaign performance — acquisition cost, conversion rate

POST-LAUNCH BOARD REPORT (Week 16)
├── CEO: 30-day post-launch report to board
│   ├── Volume vs. projection
│   ├── Member feedback and complaints
│   ├── Compliance and operational issues
│   └── Recommendation: continue / modify / withdraw
└── Board: Ratify product continuation or direct modifications
```

## Product-Specific Compliance Requirements

### Loan Products (New Consumer Loan Type)
| Requirement | Regulation | Owner | Deadline |
|-------------|-----------|-------|---------|
| Loan Estimate or TILA disclosure accurate | 12 CFR 1026 | Loan Officer + Compliance Officer | Before first application |
| Adverse action notices for all declines | 12 CFR 202 (Reg B) | Loan Officer | Within 30 days of decision |
| HMDA reporting (if mortgage-adjacent) | 12 CFR 1003 | Mortgage Processor + Compliance Officer | Annual LAR |
| Fair lending analysis | 12 CFR 202 | Compliance Officer | Pre-launch and quarterly |
| SCRA protections documented in procedures | 50 U.S.C. § 3937 | Loan Officer | In procedures before launch |

### Deposit Products (New Account Type)
| Requirement | Regulation | Owner | Deadline |
|-------------|-----------|-------|---------|
| Truth in Savings disclosure current | 12 CFR 707 / Reg DD | Deposit Ops Manager + Compliance Officer | Before first account opened |
| 30-day advance notice for rate decreases | 12 CFR 707.5 | CFO + Deposit Ops Manager | Ongoing |
| Funds availability policy updated | 12 CFR 229 (Reg CC) | Deposit Ops Manager | Before launch |
| Reg E coverage confirmed or excluded | 12 CFR 1005 | Compliance Officer | Pre-launch |
| NCUA Share Insurance disclosure | 12 CFR 740 | Member Services Rep | At account opening |

### Card Products (New Credit or Debit Card)
| Requirement | Regulation | Owner | Deadline |
|-------------|-----------|-------|---------|
| Credit card agreement accurate | 12 CFR 1026 Subpart B | Card Services + Compliance Officer | Before first card issued |
| Periodic statement disclosures | 12 CFR 1026.7 | Card Services Specialist | Ongoing |
| CARD Act compliance (credit cards) | 12 CFR 1026.51-.60 | Compliance Officer | Pre-launch |
| Reg E coverage for debit cards | 12 CFR 1005 | Compliance Officer | Pre-launch |
| Network operating rules compliance | Visa/MC network rules | Card Services Specialist | Pre-launch certification |

## Quality Gate Checklist

| # | Criterion | Owner | Status |
|---|-----------|-------|--------|
| 1 | Board approval received (motion and vote) | CEO | ☐ |
| 2 | Regulatory compliance assessment complete — no blocking issues | Compliance Officer | ☐ |
| 3 | All required disclosures drafted and approved by Compliance Officer | Compliance Officer | ☐ |
| 4 | UDAP/UDAAP review of all marketing materials complete | Compliance Officer | ☐ |
| 5 | Core system configuration tested end-to-end in test environment | Core Systems Administrator | ☐ |
| 6 | BSA/AML risk assessment for new product complete | BSA Officer | ☐ |
| 7 | Staff training 100% complete — all member-facing roles | CHRO + Branch Manager | ☐ |
| 8 | Internal Auditor pre-launch review complete — no blocking findings | Internal Auditor | ☐ |
| 9 | ALCO pricing approval in effect | CFO | ☐ |
| 10 | CEO GO/NO-GO decision documented | CEO | ☐ |

**Gate Keeper**: CEO + Compliance Officer (dual approval required)

**Gate Decision**:
- **LAUNCH APPROVED**: All criteria met — product live on approved date
- **CONDITIONAL LAUNCH**: Minor items outstanding — complete within 5 days; document plan
- **LAUNCH BLOCKED**: Material compliance or system issue — remediate and re-gate before launch

## Key Risks to Manage

| Risk | Owner | Mitigation |
|------|-------|-----------|
| UDAP violation in marketing materials | Compliance Officer | Legal review before printing; no "free" or "guaranteed" without documentation |
| Disclosure inaccuracy discovered post-launch | Compliance Officer | Compliance officer approval required on final production versions |
| Core system configuration error causes incorrect rates or fees | Core Systems Administrator | End-to-end testing in test environment before production |
| Staff cannot explain product — member confusion | CHRO + Branch Manager | Training before launch, not same day |
| Volume exceeds capacity — service degradation | CFO + Core Systems Administrator + IT Infrastructure Engineer | Growth guardrails in first 30 days; monitor daily |
| AML patterns unexpected in new member segment | BSA Officer | Monthly review of transaction patterns in first 90 days |
