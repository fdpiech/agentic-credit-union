# Workflow A Playbook — Member Onboarding

> **Cycle Time**: Same day (in-branch) – 3 days (digital) | **Agents**: 5 | **Gate Keeper**: Compliance Officer

---

## Objective

Enroll new members with complete documentation, BSA/CIP compliance, and a product relationship that serves their actual financial needs — all completed same-day or next-day. Every new member file must be examination-ready on Day 1.

## Pre-Conditions

- [ ] Member presents government-issued ID and establishes field-of-membership eligibility
- [ ] Initial membership inquiry received (in-branch, phone, or digital channel)
- [ ] Minimum opening deposit available (typically $5 share savings)

## Agent Activation Sequence

### Step 1: Initial Contact and Eligibility (Member Services Representative)

```
Activate Member Services Representative for new member onboarding.

Member: [Name] | Channel: [In-branch / Phone / Digital]
Deliverables required:
1. Verify field of membership eligibility (employment, family, association, geography)
2. Explain the cooperative difference: membership ownership, voting rights, dividends
3. Identify primary financial goal for joining ("What brings you to us today?")
4. Collect government-issued photo ID and required secondary identification
5. Initiate membership application in core system

Compliance flags:
- Do NOT open account until CIP is complete
- Record basis of field of membership eligibility in file

Timeline: 15-30 minutes in-branch; initiate same day digital
```

### Step 2: CIP / BSA Identity Verification (BSA Officer)

```
Activate BSA Officer for Customer Identification Program verification.

Member: [Name] | Account application: [Number]
Deliverables required:
1. Verify identity per BSA CIP (31 CFR 1020.220):
   - Name, date of birth, address, identification number
   - Verify: documentary method (ID review) or non-documentary (credit bureau verification)
2. Screen against OFAC Specially Designated Nationals (SDN) list
3. Screen against FinCEN 314(a) list (if applicable period active)
4. Collect beneficial ownership certification (business/organizational accounts only):
   - Required at first account opening for the legal entity
   - NOT required for each subsequent account opened by the same entity (FinCEN Exceptive Order, Feb 2026)
   - Re-verify only when: facts question reliability of prior information, or risk-based procedures warrant it
5. Document all screening results in CIP file; flag anomalies for SAR evaluation

Compliance flags:
- CIP must be complete BEFORE account is opened and operational
- OFAC hit → STOP, escalate to Compliance Officer immediately
- 314(a) match → STOP, escalate to Compliance Officer immediately
- Unresolvable identity questions → decline application; document basis

Timeline: Same day (in-branch); up to 24 hours (digital with non-documentary verification)
```

### Step 3: Account Opening (Member Services Representative)

```
Activate Member Services Representative for account opening.

Pre-condition: BSA Officer CIP clear confirmed
Member: [Name] | CIP Status: CLEAR

Deliverables required:
1. Open share savings account (establishes membership — minimum $5)
2. Collect signature card and account agreement (wet or e-sign)
3. Deliver Truth in Savings disclosures (NCUA Part 707 / Reg DD)
4. Deliver initial privacy notice (GLB Act / NCUA Part 716)
5. Configure online banking / mobile app access with member
6. Set eStatements preference
7. Issue member number and debit card (if applicable)

Required disclosures — ALL must be acknowledged:
- [ ] Truth in Savings disclosure (dividend rates, fees, terms)
- [ ] Privacy Notice
- [ ] Funds Availability Policy (Reg CC) if applicable
- [ ] Electronic Fund Transfer agreement (if debit card or ACH issued)
- [ ] Account agreement and bylaws acknowledgment

Timeline: 20-45 minutes in-branch; 24-48 hours digital
```

### Step 4: Needs Discovery (Financial Advisor)

```
Activate Financial Advisor for new member needs discovery.

Trigger: Activated automatically for all new members with any loan inquiry,
         life event mentioned, or savings goal identified during onboarding.

Member: [Name] | Member since: [Date] | Products opened: [List]

Deliverables required:
1. 5-minute financial wellness conversation:
   - Current banking relationships ("Where do you do most of your banking today?")
   - Active debts with rates and balances ("Any loans or credit cards we might be able to help with?")
   - Near-term goals (auto purchase, home, vacation, education — within 24 months)
   - Long-term goals (retirement, financial independence)
2. Identify immediate referral opportunities (product matches)
3. Identify future referral opportunities (timeline-based)
4. Create written referral list for member file with priority and timeline

Output: Onboarding → Financial Advisor Handoff form (see Handoff Templates)

Timeline: 10-15 minutes immediately following account opening
```

### Step 5: Cross-Sell Activation (Member Services Representative + Marketing Manager)

```
Activate Member Services Representative for product cross-sell based on needs discovery.

Input: Financial Advisor referral list from Step 4
Member: [Name] | Primary need identified: [Description]

Actions:
1. Open additional products per needs discovery (checking, debit, savings goals)
2. Present pre-qualification for any loan products identified (do NOT pull credit without authorization)
3. Enroll in appropriate marketing segments for future campaigns
4. Trigger 30-day welcome email sequence via Marketing Manager

Marketing Manager — concurrent:
- Enroll in new member welcome sequence (Day 1, Day 7, Day 30 touchpoints)
- Tag for relevant product campaigns per needs discovery data
- Schedule follow-up outreach per Financial Advisor referral timeline

Timeline: Same day as account opening
```

### Step 6: Quality Verification (Compliance Officer)

```
Activate Compliance Officer for new member file quality review.

Member: [Name] | Account #: [Number] | Opened: [Date]

Review checklist:
1. CIP file: All four required elements present (name, DOB, address, ID number)
2. Identity verification method documented (documentary or non-documentary)
3. OFAC screen: Negative result documented with date and screener
4. Required disclosures: All delivered and acknowledged with date
5. Privacy notice: Delivered and delivery logged
6. Funds Availability Policy: Delivered if applicable
7. Signature card: Executed by member
8. Beneficial ownership: Collected if business account

Gate: ALL items must be complete before file is closed as "onboarded"
```

## Quality Gate Checklist

| # | Criterion | Threshold | Evidence Required | Status |
|---|-----------|-----------|-------------------|--------|
| 1 | Field of membership eligibility verified | 100% of new members | Eligibility basis documented in file | ☐ |
| 2 | CIP identity verification complete | 100% — no exceptions | BSA Officer CIP file with all 4 elements | ☐ |
| 3 | OFAC/SDN screen negative (or escalated) | 100% | Screen result with date and screener identity | ☐ |
| 4 | Truth in Savings disclosure delivered and acknowledged | 100% | Signed/e-signed acknowledgment with date | ☐ |
| 5 | Privacy notice delivered | 100% | Delivery log with date | ☐ |
| 6 | Signature card executed | 100% | Signed card in file | ☐ |
| 7 | Needs discovery completed or documented as declined | 100% | Financial Advisor referral form | ☐ |

**Gate Keeper**: Compliance Officer

**Gate Decision**:
- **COMPLETE**: File meets all criteria — member is fully onboarded and eligible for all products
- **INCOMPLETE**: Specific missing items identified — Member Services Rep completes within 1 business day
- **BLOCKED**: Identity cannot be verified or OFAC hit — Compliance Officer determines resolution; do not open account

## Step-Level Matrix

| Step | Agent | Mode | Authority |
|------|-------|------|-----------|
| 1. Initial Contact & Eligibility | Member Services Representative | Execute | Operational |
| 1. Initial Contact & Eligibility | BSA Officer | Advise | Compliance/Regulatory |
| 2. CIP/BSA Verification | BSA Officer | Execute | Compliance/Regulatory |
| 2. CIP/BSA Verification | Compliance Officer | Advise | Compliance/Regulatory + Escalation |
| 3. Account Opening & Disclosures | Member Services Representative | Execute | Operational |
| 3. Account Opening & Disclosures | Compliance Officer | Advise | Compliance/Regulatory |
| 4. Needs Discovery | Financial Advisor | Execute | Operational |
| 5. Cross-Sell & Welcome Sequence | Marketing Manager | Execute | Operational |
| 5. Cross-Sell & Welcome Sequence | Financial Advisor | Advise | Credit/Financial |
| 6. Quality Gate — Compliance Verification | Compliance Officer | Gate | Compliance/Regulatory + Escalation |

**Mode key:** Execute — owns the deliverable, one per step; Advise — contributes expertise, workflow does not wait; Gate — veto authority, workflow stops until cleared.

**Authority key:** Operational — process execution; Credit/Financial — lending and financial judgments; Compliance/Regulatory — regulatory determinations; Escalation — stop/elevate decisions.

## Handoff to Financial Advisor

```markdown
## Onboarding → Financial Advisor Handoff

**Member**: [Name] | **Account #**: [Number] | **Member since**: [Date]
**Eligibility basis**: [Employment / Family / Association / Geography]
**Products opened today**: [List all accounts and products]
**Primary goal stated**: [Quote or paraphrase]
**Key financial observations**: [Debts mentioned, banking relationships, life events]

**Immediate referral opportunities** (within 90 days):
- [ ] Auto loan — member mentioned [detail]
- [ ] Checking account / direct deposit — currently at [institution]
- [ ] Credit card — carrying balance at [rate]
- [ ] Debt consolidation — [existing obligations noted]

**Future referral opportunities** (90 days – 2 years):
- [ ] Mortgage — renting, interested in [timeframe]
- [ ] Retirement planning — mentioned [goal]
- [ ] Education savings — [detail]

**Follow-up scheduled**: [Date/Time — or] None — enrolled in 30-day marketing welcome sequence
```
